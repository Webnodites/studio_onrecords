ABOUT US

Studio Onrecords is a pioneering Multilingual Music Direction, Music Production, Sound Design, Audio Post Production Recording Studio situated in Devbhoomi, Dehradun in Uttarakhand.

Studio Onrecords is a result of the vision of its Founder, Shri Amit V. Kapoor.

Music provided by Amit has always been different in tone & perspective. He has always given most importance to lyrics. Amit is an award-winning Music Director and Music Composer in the Uttrakhand music industry. He is very popular in the music industry and is best known for his soulful melodies. He is a deep and serious analyst and a very talented and perceptive music director who integrates strategy and thematic programming. Amit always keeps an eye on the current and future music trends.

As a director he possesses leadership, dependability, integrity, social orientation, perceptiveness, motivation, fluency of ideas and adaptability to carry out any music projects very successfully and with an unmatched zeal. Amit formed the Studio Onrecords in ---------- and has never looked back. Today Studio Onrecords has been part of many major projects in the country.

MUSIC VIDEOS

DHO DHO KE

Amit composed a song for ICC World Cup, 2011 named ‘DHO DHO KE’ to inspire the Indian Cricket world. With this song, Studio Onrecords paid a tribute to the Indian cricket team and wished it best of luck for the World cup. The song found its place on Star news, Star Cricket, NDTV, ETV, Sahara Samay, ANI, TV24 etc.

JAI BHARAT

An inspiring song composed was a patriotic song named ‘JAI BHARAT’ appealing to the public to lend their hands towards anti terrorist drives and maintaining world peace. This song was dedicated to India and to the martyrs who laid down their lives while safeguarding the borders of our country. The song also primarily focused on the youth with very catchy lyrics that infused nationalistic feeling and created awareness about social evils.

Teri Aan Ki Khatir

A patriotic song named ‘Teri Aan Ki Khatir’ was composed as a tribute to all our martyrs.
