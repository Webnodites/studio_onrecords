About Our Founder - Amit V. Kapoor

Amit V. Kapoor is a Music Director, Music Composer, Music Programmer, Sound Engineer, a Singer and a Theater Artist. He was born in Dehradun on 10 May 1976. He is one of the best Guitarists in Uttarakhand. He started playing guitar at the age of 8 years. He is a well known name in the industry having over 30 years of varied experience in the music industry and its various aspects.

In his younger days, the musical instrument which fascinated Amit was the Guitar. Guitar was a passion for the youth (and it still is!). Amit owes a lot of his Guitar education to Bobby Cash. At the age of 14-15 he began composing music. In his College days, he joined a band, which performed at local gigs, did small shows and performed live.

Eventually Amit went on to compose for theatre (Hindi, English and Garhwali plays) and worked with various theatre groups like:

  • Theatre Space
  • Ananya
  • Yugantar
  • Bharat Vikas Parishad
  • Garhgatha
  • Vikal
  • Ipta
  • Anubhav
  • Vatayan
  • Akhil Garhwal Sabha
  • Meghdoot
  • Shrishti
  • Udgam
  • Hiwang
  • Doon Ghati Rangmanch

R D BURMAN has been an inspiration for Amit. Amit remembers him whenever he creates something new.

Amit has worked with renowned international singers like Bobby Cash and trending national singers like Kumar Shanu, Sonu Nigum, Gurdaas Maan, Jazzy B, and Hans Raj Hans. He feels privileged to work with the folk singers of Uttrakhand like Narendra Singh Negi, Preetam Bhartwan, Gajendra Rana, Manglesh, Meena Rana etc. He had also tried to enrich the folk music of Uttarakhand through Fusion music.

Amit has got a special appreciation from BAPPI LEHRI (Renowned Music Director from Bollywood since 1970, 80s) for his unique album song ‘KAGA KAGA RE’. Amit’s has given music scores for many films including HANTYA, MASAN (Himanchali Film), GARHWALI SHOLAY, JIYA KI LAADI, GATTU AUR KALJUGI DEVTA, TERI SAU, LAU JOT KI, HIMALAY KI DHAAD, NAGRAJA-SEM MUKHEM, YAAD ALI TEHRI, HOLI KAB SUBER, AB TA KHULELI RAAT etc.
